**Company Overview**

**MOL Consolidation Services**
**MOL Consolidation Service Ltd. (MCS)** was established in 2003 to bring value to customers through innovative and customized supply chain solutions. A member of the MOL Group and part of MOL’s logistics division, MCS was founded in response to rising demand from retailers in the U.S. and Europe for PO management and specialized loading of containers at origin in order to attain higher equipment utilization. Soon after its inception, the company introduced other value-added services such as product labeling, barcode scanning, and cargo inspection.

MOL Consolidation Service (MCS), the global logistics arm of MOL Group headquartered in Tokyo Japan. We specialize in providing customers the innovative and end-to-end solution that integrate supply chain design with efficient execution. These specialized services include but not limited to Ocean Consolidation Business, Vendor Managed Inventory, Air & Sea freight management, Warehousing & Distribution, Inventory Management. Through the state-of-the-art technology system - STARLINK to provide customers visibility, real time and accurate information.

**MCS** also offers its customers the use of its proprietary logistics management system, StarLink. Developed and maintained in-house, StarLink allows customers to check on cargo 24/7 via the Internet. In addition to providing first-class supply chain services, MCS lives and breathes corporate responsibility. The company actively seizes opportunities to contribute to global economic growth and development; values an open and visible management style guided by the highest of ethical and social standards; and maintains strict operating and navigation standards in order to protect the environment.

**Services Details:**

**Ocean Consolidation:**
At MCS we make it our business to understand the intricate layers of your business. We work hard to craft tailor-made solutions with clear, measurable targets for success that are results-oriented yet scalable. Our standard Ocean Consolidation services include:

*Management Services:*
- Purchase Orders (PO) management
- Bookings management
- Vendor management
- Carrier management
- Documentation management
- EDI solutions and management
- Exceptions management

*Tracking Tools:*
- Shipment status and Track & Trace
- Advance Shipment Notice (ASN)

*Other:*
- Multi-Country Consolidation (MCC)
- FCA Trucking
- Importer Security Filing
- Monthly KPI reports

**Freight Forwarding:**
Strategic partnerships with service providers, steadfast purchasing power, and total supply chain visibility through our state-of-the-art management system, Starlink©, means world-class freight forwarding services for your ocean and air cargo. Whether freight forwarding is integral to your business or needed separately, you can rely on our team to secure you the best deals and to manage your cargo from end-to-end. We offer an array of freight forwarding services, from various ocean freight and sailing options to the ability to track and trace shipments.

**Air Freight:**
We offer a complete package of air logistics solutions services. Through our global presence, strategic alliances, and full supply chain visibility, our customers have access to a worldwide air service network. They need only to interface with a single, trusted point of contact – us.

**Warehouse & Distribution:**
*Warehouse:*
Our best-in-class warehouses are strategically located and armed with customizable tools, processes, and systems to help you grow your business. In addition, many Value Added Services are available to help you optimize your supply chain process.

*Distribution:*
Thanks to long-standing relationships with valued customers and vendors, our niche trucking services have become trusted in the industry. Partner with us for one-stop trucking services at competitive rates. Our trucking services are comprehensive, reliable, and include:
- Booking of services via the Internet.
- Coordination with customs brokers.
- Coordination with ocean carriers and depots for empty container pick-ups.
- Timely return of laden containers to ocean carrier terminals.

**Customs Brokerage:**
We understand a single customs-related oversight can disrupt the entire supply chain process. Take comfort in knowing we stand behind you in ensuring the timely clearance of your goods, while remaining in full compliance with import regulations.

**Value Added Services:**
Cherry-pick from a menu of value-added services to bolster your supply chain operation. Whether you need services on a project basis or year round, we're here to build a plan that meets your businesses' unique needs. Our value-added services include:
- Pick and Pack, down to the SKU / item level
- Inventory management and visibility
- Barcode printing, labeling, and scanning
- Slip sheeting and shrink wrapping
- Slot unloading, via our web-based cargo management system, Starlink©
- Kitting
- Repackaging / reworking
- Palletization
- QA / QC checking
