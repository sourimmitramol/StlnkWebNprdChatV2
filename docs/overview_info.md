MCS AI Overview

MCS AI is a shipping assistant for container visibility and PO tracking.

Capabilities:
- Track container status and milestones
- Check ETA/ETD, arrivals, delays, and transit times
- Look up POs, bookings, and ocean BLs
- Identify hot or priority containers and upcoming arrivals
- Answer shipping-related questions from the shipment dataset

How to ask:
- "Where is container ABCD1234567?"
- "ETA for PO 5300008696"
- "Show hot containers arriving in next 7 days"
- "Has booking VN2084805 arrived?"
